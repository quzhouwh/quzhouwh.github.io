About Me

 I'm currently a Ph.D. candidate in Natural Resources and Environmental Sciences (NRES) at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC). Before that, I received my B.E. in Remote Sensing Science and Technology in 2018 and M.E. in Computer Technology in 2020 from Wuhan University (WHU). My research focuses on using remote sensing and advanced process-based/data-driven models to retrieve agroecosystem information for global food security and agroecosystem sustainability.
